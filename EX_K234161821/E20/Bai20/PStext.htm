Product Summary

They print, scan and copy. In color.

Once again, Jackson Electronics sets the standard. This time with high-quality inkjet color printing and precision digital color scanning together in one product for one very affordable price. The perfect solution for your printing, scanning and copying needs all in one box.

  • Full size legal scanning surface.
