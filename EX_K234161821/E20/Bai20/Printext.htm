Laser Print 5000

Product Summary

Easy-to-use, value-packed printing for small businesses

The Laser Print 5000 printer advances the art of business printing, giving you lots of options to choose from as your needs evolve. With easier operation, enhanced performance, versatile paper handling and more ways to expand and connect to the network, the Laser Print 5000 printer lets you pack a lot of printing into your budget.

The Laser Print 5000 printer builds on the proven performance of the Jackson Electronics line of printers, with improvements that make printing easier and more productive. An improved control panel and updated software give you or your workgroup easier printing. And Jackson Electronics has developed a number of hardware and software performance enhancements for noticeably faster output, especially with complex documents.

Features

  • Easy-to-use control panel with simple "Go" and "Job Cancel" buttons
  • Numbered paper trays and paper-level indicators
  • A suite of hardware and software improvements, for faster performance
  • Hardware-assisted scaling, halftoning and compression of graphic images
  • New bi-directional port prints your jobs twice as fast
  • Optional paper-handling accessories, including duplex printing and additional paper capacity
  • Expandable memory up to 64 megabytes
  • Dependable 40,000 page monthly duty cycle
  • Uses the same toner cartridge as other Jackson Electronics printers
  • Jackson Electronics' warranty, reliability, service and support
